Join Our Research Community

We welcome collaboration from researchers interested in economic history, maritime history, digital humanities, and database development. Our interdisciplinary approach combines traditional historical research with cutting-edge computational methods.

Contact us to learn about opportunities to contribute to the project, access research data, or explore collaborative research initiatives.

Maritime History

Specialists in maritime commerce, trade networks, and economic history during the medieval and early modern periods.

Digital Humanities

Experts in database design, data visualization, and computational methods for historical research and analysis.

Network Analysis

Researchers specializing in social network analysis, econometric modeling, and quantitative approaches to historical data.
